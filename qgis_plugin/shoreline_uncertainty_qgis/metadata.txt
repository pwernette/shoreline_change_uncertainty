name=Shoreline Change Uncertainty
qgisMinimumVersion=3.16
qgisMaximumVersion=4.99
description=Positional-uncertainty and rate-of-change analysis for digitized shorelines (Wernette et al. 2017/2020), with no arcpy dependency.
about=Computes RMSE-based positional uncertainty (RMSE_I/RMSE_O/RMSE95), overlapping-double-buffer (ODB) and Perkal significance testing, critical-area identification, shore-normal transects with rate-of-change (EPR/LRR), professional-delineation comparison, similarity-index/significant-change rasters, Gaussian change-probability surfaces and rate-change polygons, and an optional NOAA CO-OPS water-level lookup. Full feature parity with the standalone shoreline_uncertainty Python package, reimplemented on native QGIS/GDAL APIs (no geopandas/shapely/rasterio/pyogrio).
